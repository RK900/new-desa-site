UN Photo/Kim Haughton
These pages show only the Regular Budget of the United Nations as presented in documents A/68/6/Add.1.
Peacekeeping budget and extra-budgetary budgets are not included.
The Programme Budget

The UN programme budget covers a two year period, beginning in January of an even-numbered year (e.g. 2014-2015). It offers a wealth of information about the activities of the United Nations, including the details of the programmes carried out by the UN. Get started by selecting a biennium from the tabs above!
